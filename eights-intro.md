# Introduction

Hello! Welcome to the Ourob Framework! My name is Eights Ourophoen, and this is the introduction to everything the Ourob Framework is going to be.

To start with: this framework is designed with one main and primary *purpose* in mind—to be a comprehensive document (more or less) for the pursuit and maximization of personal happiness and security. (At least so far as is achievable, I suppose.)

You can think of this framework as many things: as a guidebook, as a game (which it’s definitely designed to be—you’ll see later…), a library, a manual, an encyclopedia… 

Is it all of those? Is it none of those?—

Good question! Anyway—

It’s going to be me and my friends guiding you through the different parts of this book/framework. They’ll be in charge of their own sections—they’ll introduce themselves to you later.

We built up this framework together. And ideally, if it works right, it has been designed for use for people with low to no available energy, and to be as easy as possible to apply for anyone at all. But on the other hand, it's got a very high cieling for those who want to push and challenge themsleves to the maximum. Well... we're hoping it works out that way, at least... 

In the meantime, though, keep this in mind: your personal security and happiness (survivability and normal-flow—terms that will be explained later) are at the core and basis of this whole framework and what it's made to do, and it has been designed such that everything rationally follows from and is based upon those main points.

So! It's time for you to meet with everyone else and get on you way!

And, *Why is it called the 'Ourob Framework'*, you ask? We'll get to that later.

Now, say hello to the rest of everyone for me!
