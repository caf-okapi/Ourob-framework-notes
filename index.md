# Ourob Framework Notes

## Overview

These are working notes toward a framework exploring behavior and personality.

This site is provisional and may change as ideas develop.

## Notes

The Ourob Framework is designed to be a rational and comprehensive framework for the pursuit of personal security and happiness, with a focus on behavior and personality. It is written from the perspective of various characters who will introduce and discuss the different topics.
